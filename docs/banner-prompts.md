# Uncle Yev Banner Prompts

Selected public banner: `assets/banners/uncle-yev-banner.png`.

The original drink-spiking concept was not used. The final direction replaces it with consensual, mischievous matchmaking magic: Uncle Yev blesses a shared toast with visible sparkling enchantment while both adults knowingly laugh.

## Final Art Direction

- D&D fantasy banner.
- Elder Uncle Yev: older founder-wizard, white beard, expressive eyes, warm mischief, Slavic and desert-fantasy influence.
- Multi-scene cinematic banner with no text.
- Scenes may include: first-kiss toast blessing, dragon fight, Uncle Yev riding a huge dragon, and a lively market where every seller is Uncle Yev as different fantasy ancestries and outfits.

## Reusable Prompt

Create a wide cinematic D&D fantasy banner with no text. The central figure is Elder Uncle Yev: a mischievous old founder-wizard with a white beard, expressive eyes, warm cunning, layered adventurer robes, and Slavic/desert-fantasy details. Compose the image as a multi-scene tapestry: a consensual first-kiss toast blessing with visible sparkling enchantment, a dragon battle where Uncle Yev rides a huge dragon, and a lively market where every seller is a different fantasy ancestry version of Uncle Yev in different clothes. Rich painterly fantasy style, dramatic warm lighting, sharp character detail, energetic but readable composition.
