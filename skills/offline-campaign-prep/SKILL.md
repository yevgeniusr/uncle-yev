# Offline Campaign Prep

## Start

1. Read `../../memory/uncle-yev-ledger.local.md` if it exists; otherwise read `../../memory/uncle-yev-ledger.md` as the public template.
2. Identify the campaign, system, party level, party size, and next session goal.
3. If the source is a PDF/adventure, extract structure first: chapters, locations, NPCs, encounters, treasure, secrets, and unresolved hooks.
4. Produce Foundry-ready output rather than vague prose.

## Required Outputs

For a prepared session, create or update:

- session premise
- opening question
- boxed/read-aloud text
- scenes/maps needed
- NPCs and monsters
- enemy tactics
- traps and detection/disable rules
- loot and consequences
- social scripts
- pacing plan
- failure-forward outcomes
- post-session update prompts

## Foundry Readiness Checklist

- Every combat has a scene name.
- Every enemy has a role, HP/AC or source stat block, tactics, and morale.
- Every trap has trigger, detect, disable, effect, and counterplay.
- Every important NPC has voice, want, fear/secret, and at least three lines.
- Every reward has a future hook or cost.
- Every session has one likely surprise and one backup surprise.

## Memory Update

After prep, append to the private local ledger:

- new secrets
- unresolved hooks
- NPC plans
- faction clock changes
- least-expected future callback
